American pancakes

Description

This is a picture of Pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with the sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the eggs and milk and whisk everything into a smooth batter. Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat.
    Pour 2 tablespoons of batter into the pan for each pancake. The pancakes should be about 10 cm in diameter.

  2. Cook each pancake for about 2 minutes until small bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side for the same amount of time.
    Garnish the American pancakes with berries and maple syrup and serve.
